# 📋 Demo Build Workflow

## What is a Workflow?
A workflow is like a recipe for your AI. Sometimes, you don't want the AI to guess how to complete a task; you want it to follow a very specific, step-by-step checklist. Workflows make your AI predictable and reliable.

## Objective
This is a dummy workflow to demonstrate how you can bundle instructions. When you run `sprawl add demo_build.md`, your workspace learns this checklist.

## The Checklist:
When executing the "Demo Build", please follow these steps exactly:
1. **Say Hello:** Acknowledge that the Sprawl engine is initialized.
2. **Check the Rules:** Verify that you have read the `demo_security.md` rule and understand your boundaries.
3. **Finish Strong:** Print "Build Successful! 🎉" to the screen.

*Tip: You can create your own workflows for things like "How to review code," "How to write a blog post," or "How to summarize a meeting."*
